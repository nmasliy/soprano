Painless removal of wisdom teeth

We guarantee that the procedure will be painless thanks to the experience of our doctors and deep-acting painkillers.
When you need to remove wisdom teeth:
  1. Sharp pain in the tooth area / inflammatory process;
  2. Discomfort during chewing;
  3. The wisdom tooth presses on other teeth;
  4. Chronic injury of the cheek or tongue;
  5. the tooth began
    to break down;
  6. You are planning to straighten your teeth.
