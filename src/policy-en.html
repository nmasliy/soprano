WHAT ARE COOKIES AND WHY DO WE USE THEM?

A cookie is a small text file that is downloaded to a device or read by a browser (such as a computer, laptop, or smartphone) when a user accesses certain websites.

Almost every website uses cookie technology to optimize the design and functionality of the site. Cookies also make your interaction with websites safer and faster because they can remember your personal settings (such as login or language), sending information back to the website containing cookies or to another website to which it belongs (third-party cookies) when you revisit the website from the same device.

They allow your browser or device to be uniquely and anonymously identified.
Cookie files cannot search your computer or infect it with viruses.

Depending on the purposes for which cookies are used, they are usually divided into the following categories used by Yuriy Yuriyovych Stetsiv:

  • Necessary cookies allow you to use the website and its basic functions. They are typically set in response to your actions. These files are necessary for using our website.
  • Functional cookies are used to remember you when you revisit the site and to provide improved personalized service. For example, the website can greet you personally or remember information about your language choice or location. These cookies collect information anonymously and cannot track your visits to other websites.
  • Analytical and operational cookies allow us to store information about how you use the website and how often you visit it. For example, we store information about which pages you visit most frequently and whether you receive notifications about errors. This allows us to improve the functioning of our website, including providing fast access to important information that interests you. These cookies are only used to improve the quality of the website.
  • Targeting or advertising cookies are created to remember the number of visits to our website, as well as the list of pages you open and the links you click. These cookies are created to provide you with only relevant advertising information that corresponds to your preferences. They are used only to limit the number of times advertising messages are shown to you and to help evaluate the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

According to the storage time:

  • Persistent cookie files. They are stored in your device's web browser and are used each time you visit our site. These files help us remember your settings and preferences to make your next visit more convenient.
  • Session cookie files. These are temporary cookie files that remain in your browser cache until you leave our site.
